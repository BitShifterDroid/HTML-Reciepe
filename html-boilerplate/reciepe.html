How to make Vegetable Maggie

Ingredients required

  • Rai Seeds
  • Oil
  • Maggi noodles
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Black Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • Capsicum
  • Tomato
  • Peas

Steps to follow

  1. Take a stainless steel kadhai pour some cooking oil put it on gas and let it get warm
  2. After few seconds add rai seeds on it
  3. Take some tomatoes and grind them to get thick liquidy texture and then put some peas and capsicum cutouts in it
  4. When the rai seeds noise go off put the gas on low and put the vegies paste and some water along in the kadhai
  5. After that put maggie noodles in it and let it sit for 45 seconds
  6. After that put all the spices 1 table spoon and salt according to taste
  7. Let the noodles cook and make sure that all the water dosent evaporate if it does put more water
  8. When it looks cooked pour it out in a bowl and it goes good with a slice of bread
